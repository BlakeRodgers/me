Who is Blake Rodgers?

Born in Normal, Illinois Blake was raised in a common country midwestern home until when he was 4 when he moved to Austin, Texas. Throughout his childhood Blake was active in extracurricular activities such as chess club, soccer, taekwondo, and cub/boy scouts. Blake went on to pursue all of those activities until highschool where he stopped playing sports his sophomore year. Blake eventually went on to achieve his eagle scout and go on to pursue his associate in arts degree at Austin Community College and eventually Heartland Community College where he graduated with his degree. Blake is currently furthering his education at Illinois State University where he is the Creative Technology program.
